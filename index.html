What Is Cryptocurrency?

Cryptocurrency is a digital asset that can be transferred online without using traditional cash. Some cryptocurrencies are used as digital money, while others are used for apps, payments, or blockchain networks.

Beginner Checklist

  • Understand what a cryptocurrency is before buying it.
  • Research the purpose of the project and how the token is used.
  • Watch for news about regulation, security, and market trends.
  • Never risk money you cannot afford to lose.
