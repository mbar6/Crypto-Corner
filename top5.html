Top 5 Cryptocurrencies

Introducing the five largest cryptocurrencies by market cap without including stablecoins. Each coin aims to solve a different problem that carries risk and potential reward.

Major Cryptocurrencies

The cryptocurrency market has thousands of tokens, but very few projects receive attention. These cryptocurrencies show the different ways blockchain can solve real problems in real time.

Cryptocurrency Symbol Basic Purpose
Bitcoin BTC Digital money and store of value
Ethereum ETH Smart contracts and decentralized applications
Solana SOL Fast blockchain transactions and applications
XRP XRP Payment settlement and money transfers
BNB BNB Exchange utility and blockchain transaction fees

What Beginners Should Know

A large market cap does not automatically mean a cryptocurrency is safe. Beginners should still look at the purpose of the project, the risks, the team behind it, and how the token is actually used.
